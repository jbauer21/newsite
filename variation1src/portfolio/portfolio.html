    • Geosurfing!

    Geosurfing is an exploratory search engine designed to catalog, preserve, and educate users about the old web. The webscraper, database, backend, and front end are all built from scratch, and hosted entirely for free using Supabase and Render (with the exception of the domain, which is hosted for ten dollars a year using Porkbun).

    • COGBRE

    The Cognitive Binary Reverse Engineering Tool (COGBRE) is an XR Visualization platform that helps Binary Analyts understand malware with VR visualizations and cognitive-load reduction techniques. Our tool received significant attention from national labs, and was published in both the proceedings of IEEEVISSOFT 2024, and I/ITSEC 2024.
