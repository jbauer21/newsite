Who is this handsome devil?

Julian is a developer pursuing his M.S. in Human-Computer Interaction at the University of Maryland. He's originally from Galveston, Texas (and would be very open to moving back to the bay area!). On the side, he's a multi-musical-instrumentalist, game designer/developer, a collector of various trinkets and doodads, a part-time DJ, an avid gamer, and a die-hard independent animation fan. He's been designing and programming professionally for four years, but he's been having fun making things for over a decade. Give him a chance!
